# About Me

I am a researcher at the Research Institute for Aquaculture No.2 (RIA2),
Vietnam, specializing in fisheries ecology, aquatic biodiversity,
river monitoring, and sustainable aquaculture systems.

Currently, I am pursuing a Master degree in Aquaculture at Can Tho University.

My research focuses on:
- Fisheries ecology
- Fish migration and fish passage
- Mekong River monitoring
- Sustainable aquaculture
- Aquatic biodiversity conservation
